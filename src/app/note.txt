Directives: chỉ thị

Component Directives: Thành Phần
These form the main class having details of how the component should be processed, instantiated and used at runtime. 

Structural Directives: Cấu trúc
A structure Directives basically deals with manipulating the dom elements. Structural Directives have a * sign before the directive. 
For example: *ngIf , *ngFor. 

Attribute Directives: Thuộc tính
Attribute directive deal with changing the look and behavior of the dom element. You can create your own directives as explained in the below section.
